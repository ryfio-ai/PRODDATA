# USER MANUAL: PSG TECH STUDENT ACTIVITY PORTAL
## (TECH BULLETIN SUBMISSION GUIDE)

This manual provides step-by-step instructions for students to submit their activity records for the Mechanical Engineering Department Tech Bulletin.

---

### **Overview**
The Student Portal is designed to collect structured data for the department's annual Tech Bulletin. Students must log their international visits, co-curricular activities, awards, competitive exam results, and training/internships through this platform.

**Portal Access:**
*   **Primary Link**: [https://proddata26.vercel.app/](https://proddata26.vercel.app/)
*   **Alternate Link**: [https://ryfio-ai.github.io/PRODDATA/](https://ryfio-ai.github.io/PRODDATA/)

---

### **STEP 1: Identification**
1.  Open the portal in any modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Safari).
2.  At the top, find the **"Student Information"** section.
3.  Click the dropdown menu for **"Roll Number & Name"** and select your profile.
4.  Verify that your **Email ID** is automatically generated correctly.

---

### **STEP 2: Navigating Sections**
The portal follows a specific order of data collection (7 core categories). Click on the tab that corresponds to the activity you wish to report:
1.  **Placement Offer**: Details of company offers received (Role, Pay Package, etc.).
2.  **Higher Studies**: Enrollment details for further education.
3.  **Internship Official**: Internships arranged through the institution.
4.  **Internship Personal**: Externally sourced internships (Requires **Offer Letter** AND **Certificate**).
5.  **Papers Publication**: Consolidated section for Journal or Conference papers.
6.  **Award Data**: Individual awards and medals won.
7.  **Event Data**: Participation in events at various levels.



---

### **STEP 3: Adding Entry Records**
1.  Once you are in the correct tab, click the **"+ Add Entry"** button. This will create a form block.
2.  Fill in all the required text fields (e.g., Company Name, Event Name, Date).
3.  **Multiple Entries**: If you have participated in more than one event in the same category, click **"+ Add Entry"** again for each record.

---

### **STEP 4: Uploading Proof (Mandatory)**
You must provide a supporting document for every entry you create.
1.  Locate the **"Proof Upload"** box within your entry block.
2.  Drag and drop your file (PDF, JPG, or PNG) into the box, or click to browse.
3.  **File Size**: Ensure each file is **under 5MB**.
4.  Wait for the message: *"1 file(s) selected"* to appear.

---

### **STEP 5: Final Submission**
1.  After filling out all relevant tabs and uploading proofs, review your entries for accuracy.
2.  Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the **"Submit All Records"** button.
3.  **Wait for Confirmation**: A loading screen will appear. Do not refresh.
4.  Once the success screen (**"✓ Submission Successful!"**) appears, your data has been securely saved to the department registry.

---

### **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**
*   **Can I submit multiple categories at once?** 
    Yes, you can fill out different tabs and click submit once at the end.
*   **What if I don't see my Roll Number?**
    Please contact the department registry administrator to have your name added to the database.
*   **My file won't upload!**
    Check if the file size exceeds 5MB or if the format is not PDF, JPG, or PNG.
