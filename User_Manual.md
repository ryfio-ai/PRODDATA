# USER MANUAL: PSG TECH STUDENT ACTIVITY PORTAL
## (TECH BULLETIN SUBMISSION GUIDE)

This manual provides step-by-step instructions for students to navigate the **Sequential Wizard** and submit their activity records for the Department Tech Bulletin.

---

### **1. OVERVIEW**
The portal has been redesigned into a **guided 11-step wizard**. Instead of free-form tabs, you will follow a logical sequence to ensure all relevant professional and academic data is captured accurately.

**Mandatory Requirement:** 
Only **Step 0: Student Profile** is mandatory. All other sections (1 through 10) are optional—you should only add entries to them if you have relevant activities to report.

---

### **2. GETTING STARTED (Step 0: Profile)**
1.  **Identify Yourself**: Select your **Roll Number & Name** from the dropdown menu.
2.  **Verification**: Your institutional email will auto-populate.
3.  **Mandatory Contact Info**: You must provide your **Personal Email**, **Contact Number**, and **Current Address**.
4.  **Navigation**: Once complete, click the **"Next: Visits Abroad"** button at the bottom.

---

### **3. THE 11-STEP WORKFLOW**
You will progress through the following sections in order:
- **0. Student Profile** (Identity & Contact)
- **1. Visits Abroad** (International academic visits)
- **2. Activities** (Co-curricular & Extra-curricular)
- **3. Awards Won** (Medals, positions, and recognition)
- **4. Comp. Exams** (GATE, CAT, GRE, etc.)
- **5. Official Internship** (Institution-arranged)
- **6. Personal Internship** (Self-sourced - requires Offer Letter & Certificate)
- **7. Placement Offers** (Job offers and LPA details)
- **8. Higher Studies** (Programme and Institution)
- **9. Research Paper** (Journal/Conference publications)
- **10. Scholarships** (Financial aid, endowments, etc.)

---

### **4. ADDING & REMOVING ENTRIES**
In any section from 1 to 10:
1.  **Add Entry**: Click the **"+ Add"** button in the top right of the section card to create a record block.
2.  **Fill Details**: Provide the requested information (Semesters, Dates, Names, etc.).
3.  **Remove**: If you add a block by mistake, click the **Red X** button to delete it.
4.  **Multiple Records**: You can add as many blocks as needed within a single section.

---

### **5. UPLOADING PROOF**
Supporting documentation is required for every entry:
1.  **Max Size**: Total file size per entry must be **under 5MB**.
2.  **Formats**: PDF, JPG, or PNG only.
3.  **Placement**: Use the "Drag & Drop" zones provided for each entry. For **Personal Internships**, you must upload both an Offer Letter and a Certificate separately.

---

### **6. FINAL SUBMISSION**
1.  Navigate to the final step (**10. Scholarships**).
2.  Review all your entries using the **"Previous"** and **"Next"** buttons to ensure accuracy.
3.  Click the **"Finalize & Submit All Records"** button.
4.  **Wait**: A progress screen will appear. Once you see the **"Submission Successful"** overlay, your data is securely stored in the department registry.

---

### **7. TROUBLESHOOTING**
*   **"No Reaction" when clicking Submit?** 
    The portal will automatically jump back to the specific tab where a mandatory field (in the Profile) is missing and highlight it for you.
*   **My Roll Number is missing?**
    Contact the department administrator to update the master student list.
*   **Can I skip sections?**
    Yes! If you have no data for a section, simply leave it empty and click **"Next"**.
